Web & Graphic Design

My bachelor of arts degree project.

 Project of “Affliction” limited edition box set for polish metal band Blindead. “Affliction” is a concept album, its theme is a life of autistic girl. It contains music CDs, story book entitled “Rhythm” by Piotr Kofta and DVD with “My little Bess” movie by Roman Przylipiak.

My project includes design of: lyrics book, book with “Rhythm” story by Piotr Kofta, seven posters (one for each track), font and, of course, CDs and packaging.
